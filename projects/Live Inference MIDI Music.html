Live Inference MIDI Music
An ongoing collaboration with Thunderboom Records exploring ways to generate live MIDI music with AI, enabling musicians to jam with generative models in real time. The project combines two approaches: FIGARO Live, a real-time adaptation of the FIGARO music generation model, and a GrooveTransformer on Raspberry Pi, which runs a groove-based drum pattern generator on embedded hardware. Together, these tools let performers interact with AI-generated MIDI streams as a live musical collaborator.
